How it works

Three phases.
One destination.

Each session moves through three phases — designed to quiet the nervous system, dissolve the thinking mind, and reveal the awareness that was always here.

Used regularly, this practice reduces anxiety, improves focus (helpful for ADHD), and builds a stable inner calm that carries into daily life for adults and children alike.

1
0 – 2 minutes

Settle & Breathe

Box breathing — five counts in, five hold, five out, five rest — activates the parasympathetic nervous system, calming the anxious mind and grounding attention in the body.

2
2 – 5 minutes

Self-Inquiry

Questions from the tradition of Ramana Maharshi appear one by one. "Who is aware right now?" Not a puzzle — a mirror to look into.

3
5 – 10 minutes

Open Stillness

Nothing is asked. The orb breathes. You rest as what you are — aware, whole, and already home.
